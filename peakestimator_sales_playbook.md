# PeakEstimator Pro: Master Sales & Training Playbook

## 🧠 The Core Philosophy
To sell to blue-collar business owners (contractors, plumbers, roofers), you must respect one universal rule: **They are incredibly busy, often putting out fires, and their BS meter is highly tuned.**

You cannot sound like a typical telemarketer. You need to sound like an industry peer who has a highly valuable secret. We use a framework focused on: **Pattern Interrupt**, **Discovery (Gap Finding)**, **Pain-Killer Injection**, and a **Live Demo Close**.

---

## 📞 Section 1: The Rockstar Sales Script

### Phase 1: The Opening (Pattern Interrupt)
*Drop the "How are you doing today?" It signals a sales call. Get straight to the point and respect their time.*

> **You:** "Hey [Name], it’s [Your Name]. Look, I know I caught you right in the middle of your day, so I’ll keep this to 60 seconds. Do you have a minute, or are you under a house right now?"
> 
> *(Wait for them to laugh or say "I have a minute".)*
> 
> **You:** "I work with [Trade—e.g., Roofers/Electricians] helping them close more jobs without spending their evenings typing up estimates. Before I tell you what I do, can I ask you one quick question to see if this even makes sense for you?"

### Phase 2: Qualification & Discovery (Finding the Gap)
*Your goal here is to agitate the pain of lost time and lost bids.*

> **You:** "When you finish a walkthrough, are your guys able to build a professional quote and get it signed right there in the driveway, or are you still taking notes and typing it up at the kitchen table at 8:00 PM?"
> 
> *(If they say they type it up later or use Word/Excel):*
> 
> **You:** "Got it. And when you finally send that PDF over, how often are you chasing them down a week later, just to find out they went with the guy who gave them a price on the spot?"

### Phase 3: The Pain-Killer Pitch
*Present PeakEstimator Pro as the immediate cure to the pain discovered above.*

> **You:** "That’s exactly why I called. What if you could sit in your truck, open your phone, tap three buttons to pull your exact materials and labor rates, and instantly text the homeowner a premium, branded proposal? 
> 
> They open it on their phone, they hit 'Approve,' they sign it with their finger, and you have the green light before you even put your truck in drive. No more evening paperwork. You get your nights back, and you look like a million-dollar company. How much time would that save you every week?"

### Phase 4: The "Live Happening NOW" Close (The Rockstar Move)
*Do not ask to schedule a Zoom demo. Show them the magic right now.*

> **You:** "I don't want to waste your time with a boring slideshow. Do you have your cell phone in your hand right now?"
> 
> **Them:** "Yeah."
> 
> **You:** "What's your mobile number? I’m going to text you a live proposal right now so you can see exactly what your customers will see."
> 
> *(Instantly create a fake project in PeakEstimator Pro, click 'Share', and text them the link).*
> 
> **You:** "Click that link. See how it has your company name at the top? Scroll down. See how clean the line items are? Now, click that green **'Approve This Bid'** button."
> 
> *(They click it).*
> 
> **You:** "Boom. On my end, my dashboard just lit up green, and the job is locked in. It took you 5 seconds to approve it as the client. If your quoting process was this smooth, how many more of your leads do you think would turn into paying jobs?"

### 🛑 Crucial Objection Handling
**"We already use Jobber/ServiceTitan."**
> **Response:** "Those are great for dispatching your trucks. But they are heavy, clunky, and complicated to build a quote in. PeakEstimator Pro is a dedicated closing tool. It’s a scalpel, not a Swiss Army knife. Use them for your scheduling, use PeakEstimator when you need to actually win the $15,000 job."

## 📊 Section 2: The Pitch Deck Structure (70/30 Rule)
If they say, *"How does this work?"*, do not tell them to visit a website. Maintain momentum. 

> **You:** "It works by keeping things incredibly simple. I can get your entire team set up by tomorrow morning. I’m going to shoot you a quick 5-minute overview deck right now. Take a look, and let’s get you onboarded."

### Part 1: Emotional & Business Connection (70%)
* **Slide 1: Cover** - "Stop Writing Estimates. Start Closing Jobs."
* **Slide 2: The 8:00 PM Kitchen Table** - You didn't start a business to do paperwork at night.
* **Slide 3: The Cost of Being Slow** - The first quote usually wins. Slow quotes kill momentum.
* **Slide 4: The Pain Killer** - PeakEstimator Pro features (Pre-loaded price books, automated margins, 1-click send).
* **Slide 5: The "Wow" Factor** - Show the frictionless mobile Client Portal. 
* **Slide 6: The ROI** - Save 10+ hours a week, increase close rates by 20%, protect margins.

### Part 2: Security, Terms, and Trust (30%)
* **Slide 7: Iron-Clad Security** - 100% data ownership, bank-grade encryption. We don't sell your data.
* **Slide 8: The "Prove It First" Pricing** - $499 30-Day Setup & Trial (covers servers & onboarding). Only lock in the $8k/yr contract AFTER we prove the ROI.
* **Slide 9: Zero Friction Onboarding** - Ready in 10 minutes. Global price books pre-loaded.
* **Slide 10: Call to Action** - Secure your account and win the next job.

## 💳 Section 3: The "Boom" Payment Workflows
**The Golden Rule:** Momentum dies the second you hang up the phone. You must close and collect payment *live* while still talking to them.

### Method 1: The Automated Payment Link
*Required: Stripe Payment Link & Hosted Deck Link.*

1. **Send the Deck Live:** "I’m texting you the deck right now. Make sure it opens."
2. **The Assumptive Drop:** "To get your account spun up today, we start with a 30-day trial phase for just $499. That strictly covers our costs to securely provision your servers, load your price books, and give you white-glove support. Once you see the ROI after 30 days, we'll lock you in for the year. What’s the best email to send your activation link to?"
3. **The Link & The Silence:** "I just texted your secure Stripe checkout link. Go ahead and click that..." **Shut up and wait.** Let them pull out their card or use Apple Pay.
4. **The Boom:** *(Phone buzzes)* "Boom. Got it, John. Payment cleared. Welcome to PeakEstimator Pro. I’m firing up your account right now."

### Method 2: The Virtual Terminal (Live Card Capture)
*Use this if links are causing friction or aren't ready. This projects extreme confidence.*

> **You:** "Let's get your account spun up so you can use this on your 2:00 PM walkthrough today. I’m sitting at my admin terminal right now. Do you want to use a Visa or an Amex to get this activated?"
> 
> *(They choose Visa).*
> 
> **You:** "Perfect. Go ahead with the card number whenever you're ready." *(Type silently. Read back in blocks of 4).* "And expiration? CVC? Billing zip code?"
> 
> **You:** "Alright, I just hit process. Boom, approved. You're officially live."

*If they hesitate on security:* "I completely understand. I'm typing this directly into our encrypted Stripe gateway—the same system Amazon uses. But if you prefer, I can text you a secure invoice link right now to type it yourself."

### Method 3: The Wire / ACH Transfer
*Warning: Only use this for Annual Upfront Plans ($1k+).*

> **You:** "To get your annual license activated, we'll just do a direct wire. I’m shooting over our corporate wire details right now... Does your bank let you shoot that over from your mobile app right now, or is your bookkeeper handling that?"
> 
> *(If bookkeeper):* "No problem. Forward that email to your bookkeeper and CC me so I can authorize your account access the second it hits our ledger."
